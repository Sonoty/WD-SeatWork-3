It's AKATSUKI

The Akatsuki is a notorious and powerful criminal organization in the anime and manga series Naruto, created by Masashi Kishimoto. The group is composed of rogue ninja from various hidden villages, each possessing exceptional skills and unique abilities. The Akatsuki's primary goal is to capture all the Tailed Beasts (Bijuu) to achieve world domination and create a new world order. They are known for their distinctive black cloaks with red clouds, wide-brimmed hats, and polished nails. Key members include Pain (Nagato), Itachi Uchiha, Kisame Hoshigaki, and Konan. The Akatsuki plays a central role in the series' plot, posing significant threats to the protagonist, Naruto Uzumaki, and his allies.
